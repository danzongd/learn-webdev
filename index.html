Chen Junde

"Je me révolte, donc nous sommes."
(I rebel—therefore we exist. — Albert Camus)

Physically anchored as a student in Guangzhou, yet intellectually wandering through the agora of Ancient Greece and the bare-metal structures of modern computing. I am an architect of logic and a deconstructor of meaning.

Here, the absolute memory safety of Rust meets the infinite freedom of the conscious ego. Between decoding the rigorous syntax of Classical Latin, compiling the core of Arch Linux, and confronting the mechanisms of the Social Contract, I find my tranquillitas (peace).
